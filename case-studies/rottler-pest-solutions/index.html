The problem was not the trucks. It was the exit strategy.

Rottler Pest Solutions had a common fleet problem. When trucks aged out, the simple option was to send them back to the leasing company or move them through a low-effort disposal channel.

That option was easy, but it was expensive. In one example, Rottler received $1,500 for a truck, paid an additional $400 fee, and later saw the same truck resold for around $10,000.

That is the hidden fleet cost.

When a business accepts a low vehicle exit value, the money does not disappear. It goes to whoever controls the resale process.

What iAutoAgent handled

iAutoAgent took over the selling process so Rottler's team could stay focused on running the business. The Fleet Smart process included vehicle review, inspection coordination, detailing, photos, video, listings, buyer communication, showings, negotiation, paperwork, and final sale coordination.

The result

Over five years, iAutoAgent helped Rottler sell 36 fleet vehicles and recover more than $200,000 in value. The gain did not come from selling more trucks. It came from selling the same trucks better.
